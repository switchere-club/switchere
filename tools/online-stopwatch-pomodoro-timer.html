    The Pomodoro Technique for Productivity

    The Pomodoro Technique is a simple yet powerful time management method. Work in focused 25-minute intervals (called "Pomodoros"), separated by short breaks. This prevents burnout, improves focus, and makes large tasks feel more manageable. Our timer automates this process, so you can concentrate on your work.

    Precision with the Online Stopwatch

    Need to time a workout, a presentation, or a science experiment? Our online stopwatch offers precision down to the millisecond. The "Lap" feature lets you record split times, making it perfect for tracking performance in sports, races, or any activity with multiple stages.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions about our timer and stopwatch.

    What is the Pomodoro Technique?

    It's a time management method where you work in focused 25-minute sessions, followed by a 5-minute break. After four sessions, you take a longer break of 15-30 minutes. This cycle is proven to increase focus and prevent mental fatigue.

    Will I hear a sound when the timer finishes?

    Yes. A simple, audible beep will sound when any Pomodoro session (work or break) comes to an end, so you don't have to keep watching the clock.

    Does the timer work if I switch to another tab?

    Yes! Our timers use a reliable method to keep running in the background. Additionally, the Pomodoro timer updates the browser tab's title, so you can always see the remaining time at a glance without having to switch back.
