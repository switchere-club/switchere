Your Questions, Answered

Is my mood data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All data is stored exclusively on your device within your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever sent to a server, ensuring your reflections remain completely private.

How often should I use the mood tracker?

Consistency is more important than frequency. Many users find a simple check-in once or twice a day—morning to set an intention, evening to reflect—provides the most benefit. Find a rhythm that feels natural and supportive for you.

What if I don't see my exact mood?

The moods provided cover broad emotional states. Choose the one that feels closest to your primary feeling. The real power comes from the personalized tips and prompts that help you explore that feeling more deeply, no matter the label.
