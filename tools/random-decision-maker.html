Frequently Asked Questions

Is the choice truly random?

Yes! Our tool uses JavaScript's standard `Math.random()` function, which generates a pseudo-random number to select an index from your list of options. For all practical purposes, the selection is completely random and unbiased.

How do I enter my options?

You can enter each option on a new line or separate them with commas. The tool will automatically clean up the list, removing any blank entries, so you don't have to worry about extra spaces or empty lines.

Can I use this for serious decisions?

This tool is designed for fun, low-stakes decisions. While it provides a truly random choice, we don't recommend using it for life-altering decisions! Please use your best judgment for important matters.
