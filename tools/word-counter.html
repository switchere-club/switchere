Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our word and character counting tool.

How is the word count calculated?

We calculate words by splitting the text by spaces, new lines, and other common separators. This method provides an accurate count that aligns with standards used by most word processing software.

How is the reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on the average reading speed of an adult, which is approximately 200 words per minute. We divide the total word count by 200 to give you a close estimate of how long it will take to read your text. For texts under 200 words, it will show as a 1-minute read.

What is keyword density?

Keyword density is an SEO metric that measures the percentage of times a specific keyword or phrase appears in a text compared to the total number of words. It helps you analyze if you are using your target keywords enough, but not too often (which can be seen as "keyword stuffing" by search engines). A typical recommendation is a density of 1-2% for main keywords.

Is my text saved anywhere?

No. Your privacy is our priority. All calculations are performed directly in your browser. Your text is never sent to or stored on our servers. The moment you close the page, your text is gone.
