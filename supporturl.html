FAQs

Q: How do I use ProblemPal?

A: Open the app, take a picture of your question, and get instant answers!

Q: What subjects are supported?

A: ProblemPal supports all major subjects, including Math, Science, History, and more.

Q: Why is the camera not working?

A: Make sure you've granted camera permissions in your device settings.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues, please try the following steps:

  • Ensure you are connected to the internet.
  • Check that the app has camera and storage permissions enabled.
  • Restart the app if it is not responding properly.
